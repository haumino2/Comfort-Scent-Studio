    Understanding fabric conditioner

    Designed for a fresher laundry feel

    Comfort is formulated to help clothes feel softer and smell pleasant after washing. Results depend on load size, water hardness, and drying method.

    Scent strength that fits your routine

    Helps you choose a scent strength that fits your routine — from light everyday freshness to longer-lasting confidence for busy households.

    Right product for your fabrics

    Different variants suit different laundry mixes. This advisor matches your washing context to a recommended bottle — not a performance guarantee.

    Indoor vs outdoor drying

    Indoor drying may affect how scent develops. Products with balanced or gentle profiles are often chosen for enclosed drying spaces.
